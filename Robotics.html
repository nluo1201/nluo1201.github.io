Robotic Club at Edison High School

Member:

I was one of the members of Edison High school's Robotics Club in 2012. I was one among the 3 selected drivers to participate in the First Robotics Competition. We build a robot that was capable of avoiding obstacles, passing through ramps, and shooting basketballs. With the help from Graco's engineers, I learned to program directional components in LabView.

Mentor:

From 2014 until now, I had became one of the mentor there at Edison. I helped high school students learn how to build the robot using their existence knowledge in mathematics and physics.
